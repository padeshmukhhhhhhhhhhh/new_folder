What is Bitcoin?

In the world of cryptocurrency, the strongest one is Bitcoin, with a market cap of $1.91 trillion. The mysterious creator, Satoshi Nakamoto, introduced this currency in 2009..

Bitcoin operates on a decentralized computer network and functions mostly anonymously. You can purchase Bitcoin directly on exchange sites or earn it through mining.

Advantages of Bitcoin: 1)Anonymity 2)Security 3)Ease of transaction

Disadvantages of Bitcoin: 1)Volatility 2)Absence of regulatory oversight
